About Infinite One

Infinite One is a comic series exploring the journey of Arhiet, who crosses dimensions and realities to gain enough power to understand and become beyond the layers of linear time so he can understand his past. Follow our protagonist as they navigate through endless worlds, each with their own rules and challenges.

Created with passion and dedication by a university student, this comic aims to push the boundaries of storytelling and visual art.

New chapters are released bi-weekly. Stay tuned and support the journey!
